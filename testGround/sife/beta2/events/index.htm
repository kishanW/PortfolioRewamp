USB Flash Drive Fundraiser | May 24, 2010.
The inaugural event organized by the Franklin SIFE to help the students of Franklin University. This event toook place at the Phillips Hall's lobby in front of the library.

In this even we offered USB flashdrives for $9.99 each, which will help the students to save almost 40% to 50% of the cost they will spend buying these at a regular bookstore.

The fundraiser event continued for a two weeks time period until June 4, 2010. Students were be able to get more infomation about Franklin SIFE organization. Also students were able sign up for Franklin SIFE at this event table.
