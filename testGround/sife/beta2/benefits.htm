The Benefits

Gain Experience
Franklin SIFE provides students with excellent opportunities to gain real-world experience while working on projects that they plan, develop, and execute. Students can apply their skills based on their academic majors, and the experienced gained through SIFE is a valuable addition to any resume.


Network with Fortune 500 Companies
SIFE has many corporate sponsors in various industries, including top Fortune 500 Companies and being a SIFE member provides excellent opportunities to network with these companies.


Help the Community
SIFE projects include community outreach projects that help the community by educating people about various aspects of the business process including financial literacy, success skills, market economics, and environmental sustainability. SIFE enables students to make a difference and improve the lives of others.


Work on Exciting Projects
SIFE students come up with their own projects, and the project criteria is broad, allowing students to be creative and work on projects that best suit their education. Sponsor companies also send projects ideas from time to time and this can be a great way to network with a large corporation.


Scholarships
SIFE has several scholarships available for SIFE students. These scholarships range from a few hundred to several thousand dollars, and in recent years the success rate has been very high for earning SIFE scholarships.


Free to join
SIFE is completely free for all Franklin students, there are no membership fees.
