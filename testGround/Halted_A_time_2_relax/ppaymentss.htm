Company Policies
Payment is due the same day that services are rendered. A Time 2 Relax accepts all major credit cards, checks (with ID), and the ultimate.....cold hard cash!

There are 3 payment options available for corporations and schools.

Option 1: Employer pays 100%
Option 2: Employer & Employee split
Option 3: Employees pay 100%

If option 2 or 3 is selected, the individual responsible for payment, shall first pay the practitioner 100% of the money due, then collect the percentage owed by the employees.
