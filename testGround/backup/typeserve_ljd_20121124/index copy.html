About Us

I view my job as a photographer as one of the most important yet mysterious on Earth – without this medium I wouldn’t know what my Grandmother looked like as a child or remember the tears on my husband’s cheek on our wedding day as clearly. Pictures are so common today that we forget what a modern miracle they really are. Having the power to stop time in its tracks continuously drives me to create this miracle for my clients.

Our Philosophy

I believe that photography is both an art and a science and I work tirelessly to ensure that my clients love both the creative and technical aspects of what they see. It is my mission to make my clients’ experience from shoot to print both effortless and seamless so that I might have the privilege of being their lifetime photographer of choice.
