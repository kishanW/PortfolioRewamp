PathWell Enterprises provides assembly automation solutions, quality confirmation and test systems, and numerous engineering services to a variety of different manufacturing industries including automotive, medical, business equipment and consumer products. We offer total turnkey systems, build to print, design for customer builds, process engineering services and consulting to meet the individual needs of our customers.

At PathWell we understand that in this highly competitive business environment companies must closely monitor all expenditures and cut costs wherever possible. We have taken many steps to reduce waste in all areas our operation in order to provide our customers with the most value for their investment.

Combining the industries latest proven cutting edge technologies with our experience and innovative, "out side the box", thinking, PathWell is dedicated to providing our customers with the highest quality state of the art products and services at the most competitive price delivered on time.
