Steps to make the artwork:

1.)           Separating the bird from the background:

a.)           Used the Quick Mask Mode to select the needed area of the picture of the bird.

b.)          Used the menu command:

Select > Inverse

To select the inverse selection from the selection I made from the Quick Mask mode.

c.)           Copy the selection to a new layer.

2.)           Adding the selected bird to the selected background.

3.)           Adding the bird a shadow:

                                a.)           Make a copy of the layer of the bird.

                                b.)          Select the inverse of the bird by using the Magic Wand tool.

                                c.)           Add the effect Feather to the selection.

                                d.)          Fill the layer with 100% black.

                                e.)          Adjust the black layer’s position to fit the lightning of the background layer.

f.)           Adjust the opacity of the shadow layer to fit the lightning of the background layer.

4.)         Adjust the Levels and colors of the entire picture to suit the eye.
